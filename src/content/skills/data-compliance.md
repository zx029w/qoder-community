## Use Cases

- Compliance consulting for data processing scenarios with actionable conclusions
- Structured risk assessment based on a 7-dimension model (data type, processing stage, cross-border, de-identification, scale, automated decision-making, third-party sharing)
- PII offline data table selection guide with regional routing
- Compliance review and permission application workflow guidance
- Marketing compliance for EDM, phone, WhatsApp, SMS, in-app messaging
- Impact assessments (PIPIA/DPIA/PIA) with trigger conditions and templates
- Data lifecycle management: subject rights, retention/deletion, breach response, DPA
